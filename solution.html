    Activities and Norms

  • Construction and maintenance of physical infrastructure of PHCs.
  • Construction and maintenance of physical infrastructure of CHCs.
  • Selection, training and remuneration of Specialists at CHC level.
  • Selection, training and remuneration of Pharmacists at CHC level.
  • Selection, training and remuneration of Medical Officers at PHCs.
  • Selection, training and remuneration of Staff Nurses at PHC/CHC level.
  • Visioning workshops for National, State, District and Block level Mission Teams.

    Implementation Strategies

  • Train and improve the capacity of Panchayati Raj Institutions (PRIs) to own, control and manage all the public health services.
  • Technical Support provided to the National, State and District Health Missions, for the Public Health Management.
  • Regulation of the Private Sector including the informal rural practitioners to ensure the availability of quality service to the citizens at the reasonable cost.
